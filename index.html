GIS & Data Visualization Specialist

Summer 2022 – Present

Greenpeace USA

Maintained GIS datasets, analyzed and disseminated spatial data for teams across Greenpeace. The position supported the Data Strategist & GIS Lead in collecting requirements and developing project plans for Development and Programs teams.

Developed data visualizations, mapping applications, and tools for various stakeholders. Such deliverables required custom scripts with Python 3.x for GIS tasks with ArcPy, PANDAS, and ArcGIS API for Python. Developed backend automated data workflows for several teams.

Extensively used ESRI suite and Tableau to produce web layers and maps for teams across the organization. Explained GIS technical concepts to non-technical users in a comprehensive and clear fashion. Additionally, led GIS projects involving multiple stakeholders and created GIS team documentation.

Biodiversity Fellow

Spring 2020 - Summer 2021

Yale Office of Sustainability

Coordinates with Yale Facilities and faculty members to develop a high-level Biodiversity Plan for campus. The plan will identify priorities for vegetation, fauna, research, education, and carbon sequestration, and provide information to support standards for landscape design and management.

Coordinated with Yale Facilities and faculty members to develop a high-level Biodiversity Plan for the campus. The plan helped identify priorities for vegetation, fauna, research, education, and carbon sequestration and provided information to support standards for landscape design and management.

Collected and organized data pertinent to assessing campus biodiversity, setting baselines, and establishing priorities and targets. In consultation with Facilities and expert consultants, developed standards to inform design, installation, and maintenance of rain gardens, urban meadows, and swales.

Managed and updated the Office’s ArcGIS Online account and served as a resource about GIS topics. Converted CAD files to create a GIS map of the Yale campus, led research assistants to develop a campus land cover type shapefile using data collected in Field Maps, and created a Hub for future data collection.

Created downspout disconnection web maps used in the Yale Stormwater Management Plan. Worked to design visual explainers for implementing biodiversity best practices on campus and provided feedback on educational blog posts.

Student Researcher

Summer 2019 - Fall 2019

Yale Institute for Biospheric Studies

Conducted research on the effects of landscaping decisions on campus biodiversity; created recommendations for rain garden and swale installations on campus, rain garden and swale recommended plantings list, created soil testing protocols, and presented findings to various stakeholders.

Plata Lab Assistant

Summer 2016

Department of Chemical Engineering

Assisted with research on the effects of nearby fracking on the tap water in a rural Ohio community. Tested tap water samples and controls taken in the community and analyzed water samples for 16 volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and gasoline-range organic compounds (GRO) using Gas Chromatography and Liquid-Liquid Extractions.
